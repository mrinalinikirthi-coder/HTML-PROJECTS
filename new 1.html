English is a language spoken by around 1.53 billion in the world with native speakers about 400 million and second- laguage speakers nearly 1.1 billion.With the language being spoken pan world,there's absolutely no doubt about the richness of novels in it.Shall we step in the quiet layers beneath?

English Authors:

    Early Authors:
  • Caedmon - humble herdsman and earliest known poets
  • Venerable Bede - a monk and historian who was the one among people who shaped english
  • Geoffrey Chaucer - known as father of english poetry
  • William Shakespeare - known for his brilliant works in english drama plays and defined a new dimension for english literature
    Authors of Goden Age:
  • Alexander Pope - inventor of heroic couplet
  • Charles Dickens - writer who strongly expressed societal cruelty through his words
  • William Wordsworth, John Keats, Percy Shelley, Lord Byron - the romantic quartet known for their love towards nature and human emotions
    Modern Authors:
  • JK Rowling - creator of Harry Potter series
  • Ruskin Bond - known for writing feel-good stories that express deep human emotions and connections to nature
  • Julian Barnes - known for stories which reflect memory,philosophy and time
